Score predictor

Web app allowing Sale Sharks rugby fans to guess the score of a game. Correct scores were entered into a draw to win a TV from the club's principal sponsor AO.com.

About the game

The app was designed to help Sale Sharks increase matchday engagement, improve the quality of the email database and provide visibility to the new headline sponsor. Over 2000 users played the game during a 4 match span.
